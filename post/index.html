Biography

Sun, Jan 1, 2017

I’m a Bioinformatics doctoral candidate at the University of British Columbia under the supervision of Dr. Colin Collins and Dr. Cenk Sahinalp. I’m stationed at the Laboratory for Advanced Genome Analysis (LAGA), Vancouver Prostate Center. I’m working in conjunction with Lab for Bioinformatics Algorithms and Computational Genomics at Simon Fraser University/Indiana University. I reveived my undergraduate training (B.Tech) in Biotechnology from Kathmandu University, Nepal (2009). After graduation, I spent a couple of years working at The Center for Molecular Dynamics Nepal (CMDN) before moving to Canada for my PhD.
